ABOUT THE SYSTEM

THE COMPUTER INVENTORY SYSTEM IS DESIGNED TO HELP ORGANIZATIONS MANAGE THEIR HARDWARE RESOURCES EFFICIENTLY. IT ALLOWS ADMINISTRATORS TO STORE INFORMATION ABOUT COMPUTERS, MANAGE HARDWARE COMPONENTS, TRACK THEIR STATUS, AND PERFORM OPERATIONS SUCH AS ASSIGNING OR TRANSFERRING COMPONENTS BETWEEN COMPUTERS.

KEY FEATURES

COMPUTER MANAGEMENT

MANAGE AND TRACK ALL COMPUTERS WITHIN AN ORGANIZATION.

COMPONENT TRACKING

STORE AND MANAGE HARDWARE COMPONENTS SUCH AS CPU, RAM, SSD, AND GPU.

SMART SEARCH

SEARCH COMPONENTS BY TYPE, MANUFACTURER, INVENTORY NUMBER, OR STATUS.

INVENTORY OPERATIONS

ASSIGN COMPONENTS TO COMPUTERS, TRANSFER THEM, OR REMOVE THEM FROM SYSTEMS.
